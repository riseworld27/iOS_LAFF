<b>OFFICIAL HOST VENUE & PARKING INFORMATION</b>

<a href="http://www.lalive.com/">L.A. LIVE</a> is the official host venue of the 2015 Los Angeles Film Festival.
We’ve made Festival parking at L.A. LIVE easy, with a discounted rate of just $10. Enter at GATE B (on Chick Hearn Court near Georgia St) of the West Parking Garage (Lot W) at L.A. LIVE.


<b>OFFICIAL FESTIVAL PARKING AT L.A. LIVE</b>

West Parking Garage — Lot W, Gate B

1005 Chick Hearn Court (near Georgia St.)

$10 per vehicle per day — Cash only. Pay in advance. No in-and-out privileges.

PLEASE NOTE: Gate B opens one hour prior to the first Festival screening/event of the day. Other entrances to the West Parking Garage (Lot W) and any other L.A. LIVE lots are subject to the regular/posted rate(s). Special Festival pricing valid June 10 through 18 only.

<a href="http://www.lalive.com/visitor-center/parking#map">View expanded parking map</a> at www.lalive.com.
